Bacon Wrapped Scallops

Description

Bacon-wrapped scallions are a delicious and savory appetizer that combines the smoky, crispy goodness of bacon with the mild, slightly sweet flavor of fresh scallions. This dish is made by wrapping whole scallions or scallion bundles in thin strips of bacon and then cooking them until the bacon is crispy and golden brown. The contrast between the crunchy bacon and the tender, slightly caramelized scallions creates a flavorful bite with a balance of saltiness and freshness. Often grilled, baked, or pan-seared, bacon-wrapped scallions can be enjoyed on their own or paired with a dipping sauce, such as a tangy soy glaze or a creamy garlic aioli, making them a perfect addition to any appetizer spread or barbecue.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 12 sea scallops, rinsed and drained
  • 3 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cut bacon slices in half and wrap each half around a scallop. Use a toothpick to secure in place. Drizzle lemon juice over scallops. Place on a cookie sheet.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until bacon is cooked. Serve warm.
